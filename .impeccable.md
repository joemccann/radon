## Design Context

### Users

Radon serves **active options traders** who manage their own capital. They are technically sophisticated operators — not retail users seeking simplification, and not institutional desk traders buried in Bloomberg. They want to see the data, understand the methodology, and make decisions with full context. The job: surface convex opportunities from dark pool flow, vol surfaces, and cross-asset positioning, then size and execute with discipline.

### Brand Personality

**Precise. Calm. Scientific.**

The interface should evoke **calm authority** — the quiet confidence of a well-calibrated instrument that simply works. No anxiety, no hype, no urgency theater. The operator trusts the system because every metric, threshold, and signal state is inspectable.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone:** Instrument-grade terminal. Panels are modules in a measurement system, not cards in a dashboard. Hairline borders, matte surfaces, no shadows, no glassmorphism.

**References:** Oscilloscope readouts, Bloomberg's density without its chaos, research-grade scientific visualization. The Radon transform itself — reconstructing hidden structure from projections.

**Anti-references:**
- SaaS dashboards: generic card grids, rounded corners everywhere, pastel gradients, corporate feel
- Retail trading apps: gamified, confetti, hiding information behind simplification
- Crypto/fintwit: neon colors, aggressive animations, hype-driven copy

**Theme:** Dark mode primary. Light mode supported as adaptation, not equal citizen.

### Design Principles

1. **Instrument, not dashboard.** Every panel is a calibrated module. Visual weight comes from data hierarchy, not decoration. If a Bloomberg terminal is a wall of undifferentiated noise, Radon is a tuned oscilloscope — dense but purposeful.

2. **Signal clarity over P&L.** Colors communicate data quality, regime state, and structural conditions — not profit/loss. Teal means recovered structure. Amber means incomplete confidence. Red means operational fault. This is a measurement system, not a scoreboard.

3. **Mono for machines, sans for humans.** Numbers, telemetry, timestamps, and commands use IBM Plex Mono. Product voice, descriptions, and narrative use Inter. Sohne is reserved for display-weight headlines. Never reversed.

4. **Calm authority, not anxious urgency.** Animations are slow and analytical (8s scan line, 320ms fade-in). No bounce, no shake, no pulse except for genuine live-feed indicators. The system radiates confidence through stillness and precision.

5. **Operator control is non-negotiable.** Every metric shows its source. Every threshold is documented. The UI helps the trader reason and execute — it never asks for blind trust.

### Spacing & Grid

- Base unit: 4px
- Panel padding: 16-20px
- Component gap: 12-16px
- Section gap: 24-32px
- Section vertical rhythm: 56-96px
- Max content width: 1440px
- Border radius: 4px panels, 999px pills. Never rounded-lg/xl.

### Accessibility

- WCAG AA compliance (contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader basics)
- Focus rings: 2px with focus color at 60% opacity
- No reliance on color alone for state communication — text labels accompany color
